How Goody’s® Rewards Work

It has never been easier to enjoy Goody’s® rewards and privileges!

From scanning and uploading your purchase receipts to watching our latest video on YouTube, there are many ways to earn points and a variety of unique prizes, including coupons, gift cards, and more.

When you've earned enough points for the reward of your choice, head over to the catalog and click on the "REDEEM" button and follow the instructions. If you don't see "REDEEM", it just means you don't have quite enough points for that reward.
